#  AGENT PERFORMANCE & COMPLAINT RESOLUTION ANALYTICS – POWER BI DASHBOARD
A Dashboard Showing The Performance And Roles Of Various Agents In An IT Support firm Over A Period of Time.

## Project Overview
This project monitor, analyze, and optimize customer service agent performance in resolving complaints and 
technical issues over a multi-month period, using Power BI as the central analytics and reporting tool.

---
## Project Objectives
This project tracks individual and team-level performance across a one year period, focusing on key stages 
of the complaint resolution lifecycle: ticket receipt, response time, resolution time, escalation rate,
and customer satisfaction (CSAT).

---
## Key Metrics Analyzed in Power BI:
- Resolution Efficiency: Average Resolution time (days), first contact resolution (FCR) rate, and backlog
clearance rate.
- Quality of Resolution: Average Customer Satisfaction rate (CSAT) and CSAT per Agent.
- Agent Roles: Comparing agent performances across various individual specialty/roles.
- Trends Over Months: Monthly performance shifts, peak complaint volumes, and seasonal resolution bottlenecks.
- Total Number of Tickets: Amount of tickets submitted/reported.
- Compliance Rate: Service level Agreement between provider and customer.

## Key Insights
- Tickets was at an all time low of -11% in June and surged at an all time high of 14% in July.
- January recorded the highest rate of resolved ticket at 53.44% and June the lowest at 44.75%.
- SLA Compliance rose to 19.15% in September and dropped to 14.14% the following month.
- The most reoccuring issue came from bills/payment.
- Agent Sola Ogunleye had the lowest rate of resolved ticket eventhough she handled the most tickets.

re occuring issues, and assessed the performance of every agents.
